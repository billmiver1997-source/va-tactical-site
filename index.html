About VA Tactical

A sourcing layer, not a warehouse.

VA Tactical connects European buyers with tactical and field equipment manufacturers in Poland, Germany and the wider EU — without ever holding stock ourselves. Every order routes straight from the maker's warehouse to your door.

We're built on the same wholesale relationships used by established EU distributors — Military Range, Military.eu and direct manufacturer accounts — with one difference: no dead inventory, no warehousing overhead, and pricing that reflects it.

Compliance first

Every SKU is checked against EU dual-use export rules before it's listed.

No stock, ever

We never buy ahead of demand — every order triggers its own fulfilment.

Built for scale

From single field kits to IB desk and reseller volumes, on the same infrastructure.
